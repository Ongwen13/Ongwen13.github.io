VISION

Building a Society where every woman and person with disability attains the right to protection, survival, participation and development.

Featured Cause

  • To promote the observance of rights of women and persons with disabilities towards creating attitudinal change on household and community level and removing stigmas around women and Persons with Disabilities.

  • To provide and support implementation of reasonable accommodation to enable women and persons with disabilities enjoy equal rights in the society.

  • To promote women and person with disabilities accessibility to quality services towards improving their livelihoods.

  • To strengthen coordination with Government, Civil Society Organization and other NGOs on gender and disability inclusion..

About Us

Foundation of Women and Disability Inclusion (FOWADI) Uganda is a Community Based Organization in Lira Uganda since 2022. We are legally registered and licensed by the government of the Republic of Uganda. FOWADI Uganda works to empower women and persons with disabilities in society to enjoy their full rights and participate in socio-economic opportunities.
